Harmony Haven Job Advertisement


Position:

  • Program Coordinator

Are you ready to embark on a journey of compassion and change? Join us at Harmony Haven Foundation, where every day is an opportunity to make a lasting impact on the lives of underprivileged youth in our community.


Position Description:


At Harmony Haven Foundation, we don't just offer a job; we offer a chance to be part of something meaningful. As a Program Coordinator, you'll step into a role that's more than just a list of tasks - it's a narrative of empowerment, hope, and resilience.


Harmony Haven Foundation is a place where dreams are nurtured and futures are built. We are a passionate community of individuals committed to creating brighter futures for underprivileged youth. Our mission is to provide unwavering support and essential resources to local foster care programs, empowering these young lives to thrive against all odds.


What we're like:

  • Compassionate:

    We believe in the power of compassion to change lives.

  • Diverse:

    Our team represents a rich tapestry of backgrounds and experiences.

  • Innovative:

    We're always seeking fresh ideas to make a greater impact.

  • Collaborative:

    Together, we're a force for positive change in our community.

What it's like to work here:


At Harmony Haven Foundation, you'll find more than just colleagues; you'll find a family. We value collaboration, diversity, and innovation. Every day, you'll have the opportunity to contribute to a cause that truly matters. We celebrate our successes together and support each other through challenges. Your work here will be rewarding, both personally and professionally.


Salary Range:


Competitive and commensurate with experience.


How to apply:


If you're excited about our mission and ready to make a difference, we invite you to apply for the Program Coordinator position. Please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your relevant experience and why you want to be a part of Harmony Haven Foundation. Email your application here.


Diversity Statement:


Harmony Haven Foundation is committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace. We welcome and encourage applications from candidates of all backgrounds and experiences. We believe that diversity enriches our organization and contributes to our ability to make a positive impact in our community.


Join us in creating a harmonious world for underprivileged youth. Your journey starts here.
